Manhattan Mastery

A sophisticated and smooth classic.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye Whiskey (or Bourbon)
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Cherry, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well-chilled (about 30 seconds).
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
  4. Garnish with a cherry.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
