Classic Whiskey Sour

A perfectly balanced sweet, sour, and strong cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 0.75 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 0.75 oz Simple Syrup
  • 1 Egg White (optional, for a frothy texture)
  • Angostura bitters, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white (if using) in a shaker without ice.
  2. "Dry shake" vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white.
  3. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled (about 15-20 seconds).
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe or Old Fashioned glass with fresh ice.
  5. Garnish with a few dashes of Angostura bitters on top (optional).
  6. Serve and enjoy!
