Refreshing Mojito

A vibrant and zesty Cuban classic.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 2 tsp Granulated Sugar (or 1 oz simple syrup)
  • 6-8 Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Soda Water
  • Lime slice and mint sprig, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Gently muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in a highball glass.
  2. Add rum and fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top with soda water.
  4. Stir gently to combine.
  5. Garnish with a lime slice and a fresh mint sprig.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
