The Perfect Old Fashioned

A timeless cocktail, simple yet profound.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube (or 1/2 tsp granulated sugar)
  • 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • A splash of plain water (optional)
  • Orange peel, for garnish
  • Large ice cube

Instructions:

  1. Place the sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Add the bitters directly onto the sugar cube.
  3. Add a splash of water (if using) and muddle the sugar until it dissolves into a paste.
  4. Pour in the whiskey.
  5. Add a large ice cube.
  6. Stir gently for about 30 seconds until well-chilled.
  7. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink by twisting it, then drop it into the glass.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
