Classic Martini

The epitome of cocktail elegance.

Ingredients:

  • 2.5 oz Gin or Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Dry Vermouth
  • Lemon twist or olives, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. For a stirred martini: Combine gin/vodka and vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled.
  2. For a shaken martini: Combine gin/vodka and vermouth in a shaker with ice. Shake until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist or olives.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
